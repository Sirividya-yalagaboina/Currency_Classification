Currency Information

Real Currency

Real currency is an officially issued form of money that is recognized as legal tender by a government. Examples include the US Dollar (USD), Euro (EUR), and Japanese Yen (JPY).

Fake Currency

Fake currency refers to counterfeit money, which is produced illegally and designed to imitate real currency. Using or distributing fake currency is a criminal offense.

Currency Comparison

The primary difference between real currency and fake currency is legitimacy. Real currency is authorized by a governing body, while fake currency lacks official endorsement and can lead to legal consequences for those who use it.
